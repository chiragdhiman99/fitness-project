Best exercise for beginners

Bridge exercise


The bridge exercise is a fundamental movement that targets the glutes, hamstrings, and lower back. To perform it, lie on your back with knees bent and feet flat on the floor, hip-width apart. Place your arms at your sides. s.


Engage your core and lift your hips towards the ceiling, forming a straight line from your shoulders to your knees. Hold for a few seconds, then slowly lower your hips back to the starting position. This exercise strengthens the posterior chain, improves core stability, and can help alleviate lower back pain.

Knee pushup exercise


The knee push-up is an excellent exercise for building upper body strength and improving core stability, particularly for those who may find traditional push-ups challenging. This modified version of the standard push-up allows you to gradually build strength in your chest, shoulders, triceps, and core.
